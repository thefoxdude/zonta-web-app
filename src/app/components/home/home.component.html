About Us

Zonta Club of Riverside, charted in 1930, is a member of Zonta International, a worldwide service organization of members working together to empower women through service and advocacy. Over the past 10 years, our club has donated over $100,000 towards community and international service projects and scholarships in addition to thousands of hours towards hands-on projects.

VISION

Zonta International envisions a world in which women's rights are recognized as human rights and every woman is able to achieve her full potential. In such a world, women have access to all resources and are represented in decision making positions on an equal basis with men. In such a world, no woman lives in fear of violence.
