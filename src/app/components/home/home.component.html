About Us

Founded in 1938, the Zonta Club of Sacramento is a member of Zonta International, a worldwide service organization of members working together to advance the status of women through service and advocacy. Our members are dedicated to serving and making a difference in our community. The Zonta Club of Sacramento has funded numerous projects over the years. Most recently, our club has funded the “Zonta Girl” scholarships for high school seniors who demonstrate leadership in school and community activities and plan to pursue a college degree.

Donate (Support Us)

The Zonta Club of Sacramento is a 501(c)4 non-profit organization. Contributions to the club are not tax deductible. However, support for our club and club sponsored events is gratefully appreciated. Any funds received are used primarily for service projects that advance the status of women through local education scholarships, support for like-minded charitable organizations, and contributions to Zonta International Foundation service projects.

VISION

Empower and inspire women to achieve independence and freedom from violence through service and advocacy projects is paramount. We believe that providing scholarships, supporting and promoting relationships with other local community organizations focused on helping women and their families, enables women to improve their lives.
