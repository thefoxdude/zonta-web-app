Zonta Membership

As a member of Zonta, you will advance the status of women through service, advocacy and awareness. You will work make a difference in the lives of women and girls in Riverside and beyond through our service projects, scholarships and grants. While doing these activities, you will build relationships in this community and beyond that will last a lifetime.

Zonta membership provides the opportunity to:

  • Socialize and network locally, nationally and internationally with experienced, involved professionals working in many different fields, who seek to help women reach their potential.
  • Work together, through service and advocacy, towards common goals, i.e., the objectives of Zonta.
  • Want to meet interesting, involved persons who seek to help women reach their potential.
  • Experience the warmth of international fellowship. Members are welcomed at Zonta clubs worldwide.
  • Develop leadership skills and promote personal growth and self-confidence by participating actively in committees and accepting leadership roles on boards in a friendly, supportive atmosphere.

Membership Responsibilities

  • Pay all dues, fees and assessments promptly.
  • Attend club and club events.
  • Participate actively in meetings and deliberations and accept majority decisions.
  • Contribute to fundraising events (sell and/or purchase tickets, work, provide food items as requested).
  • Socialize, enjoy networking, and contribute to enhancing the status of women locally, nationally, and world-wide.
  • Accept leadership roles and give time, talent and enthusiasm to the organization.
  • Identify and suggest prospective new members.
  • For more information email riversidezonta@gmail.com or call (951) 201-8702
